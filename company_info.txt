ABOUT PORT OF TANJUNG PELEPAS (PTP)
PTP is Malaysia's most advanced container terminal.
It is located at the confluence of the main east-west shipping lanes.
PTP offers shipping lines and box operators minimal deviation time, making it a strategic transshipment hub.

KEY FACTS & FACILITIES
PTP has state-of-the-art facilities equipped with the latest technology to ensure efficient container handling.
It has a deep draft, allowing it to accommodate the world's largest container vessels.
It is situated in a free zone, offering various incentives to businesses.
PTP has excellent road and rail connectivity to the hinterland.

VISION
To be the preferred port of choice in Southeast Asia.

MISSION
To provide reliable, efficient, and cost-effective port services to our customers.
